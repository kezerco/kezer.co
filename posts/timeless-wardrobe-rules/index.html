Fashion of a Gentlemen.

I could give you exact list like 1 pair of brown dress shoe, 1 pair of black dress shoe etc.. But I do not like that kind of lists. So I will give you the basic rules to be easily in a good standing.

  1. Autumn/Winter: Dark colored shirt –> slacks should be darker

  2. Spring/Summer: Pastel shirt –> slacks should be tan or white.

  3. Solids for shirts, solids for pants

  4. Socks should never call attention to themselves

  5. Fit is everything

  6. Beware of static electricity making a mess of your entire look

Classic, timeless pieces are where every G should start. Also all of you should start thinking about wardrobes as an investment.
