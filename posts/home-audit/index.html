Here are some self audit stuff you can do around your home easily.

Summary:

  • Avoid chemicals
  • Avoid radiation

Long version:

Wifi - EMF

  • Use shielded ethernet cable like this one Once you do this, it is best to turn off Wi-Fi in your modem.
  • Have proper grounding, if you do not have call a good electirician.
  • Keyboard/Mouse: Best to use and old vintage ball wired mouse. For keyboard have a nice simple wired keyboard few inches away from your laptop.
  • Turn off Wi-Fi before bed, it is best to use timer plug as well.

Clothing

  • Only wear organic cotton shirt. This one is really simple and good quality. Generally I buy in bulk from here.
  • Blue light blocking glasses can be nice if you spend so much time in front of screen.
  • Organic cotton bedsheets
  • In kitchen only use steel, ceramic and cast iron. Nothing else.

Cleaning

  • Only thing you will ever need is vinegar and hot water. You can clean yourself, your hands, dishes and everything.
  • But if you really would love some soap, you can have pure olive oil soap like this.
